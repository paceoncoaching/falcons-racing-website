Create a modern, professional website for Falcons Pedal Mafia Racing, one of only two Western Australian teams competing in the Elite Road National Championships and Provelo Super League (PSL). The site should reflect the team's core values of Development, Mentorship, Community, and Performance.
Team Background:

Elite road cycling team based in Perth, Western Australia
Focus on developing next generation of WA cyclists
Unique culture combining experienced elite riders with emerging talent
"One in, all in" philosophy - community-driven approach
Recent achievement: Won overall 2025 Tour of Margaret River
Competing in Cycling Australia National Road Championships
Target: Top-5 finish in ProVelo Super League 2026
Long-term goal: Representation at 2032 Olympics
